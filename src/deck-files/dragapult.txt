Name: Dragapult ex
Description: Dragapult is an absurdly good deck now, and will be even better post-rotation!
- Dragapult ex is your main attacker. Place damage counters around the board and take big knockouts with Phantom Dive.
- Dusknoir synergizes great with Dragapult, allowing you to pop a bunch of damage onto an opposing Pokémon.
- Pelipper fills in for Lumineon, allowing you to grab a supporter from deck (or discard!)
- Budew slows the game down a lot. Take advantage of it if you don't get the best setup.
