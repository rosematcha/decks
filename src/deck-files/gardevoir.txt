Name: Gardevoir ex
Description: This is a combination of a lot of Japanese Gardevoir lists. Gardevoir loses Refinement, but it doesn't lose everything. Notably, it gains Clefairy, which allows it to hit for weakness on Dragapult and Raging Bolt. The deck now also leans a lot more on Research to get those Psychic energy into the discard and cuts the TM Evolution since you don't really want Kirlias on your board.
