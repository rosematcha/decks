Name: Flareon ex Toolbox
Description: Flareon feels like a worse Tera Box, but it's also a fun deck. Most of the sauce comes from the fact you can evolve the 50HP Eevee into a Flareon ex turn 1 going second, then Crispin for Burning Charge. Carnelian swings for a nice 280 for bigger threats. Sylveon's Angelite is incredibly funny, and you can use Magical Charm to KO a Dragapult thanks to Clefairy. With the Noctowl search engine, the deck is able to pull off crazy combos more often than you'd think!
